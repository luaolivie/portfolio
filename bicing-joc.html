About the Original App


What is Bicing Joc?


Barcelona Bicing Joc is a free app for people who use the city's public bikes, called Bicing. It's a gamification where you earn points for riding the bikes. You get points for things like how far you ride, how many calories you burn, and how much CO2 you save.

You can then use your points to get cool stuff like free Bicing rides, merchandise, or tickets to places like Tibidabo.

It could make using Bicing more fun and encourages riders to take bikes more often!

User Intro


Shifted from a static data display to a dynamic, motivating welcome page.
